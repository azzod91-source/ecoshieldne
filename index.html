Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about lead abatement and our services

What is asbestos abatement?

Asbestos abatement is the professional removal or encapsulation of asbestos-containing materials using EPA-approved methods. It's essential for buildings constructed before the 1980s to protect against asbestos exposure and health risks.

How do I know if my property has asbestos or mold?

Buildings constructed before the 1980s likely contain asbestos materials. Mold can develop anywhere with moisture issues. Professional testing is the only way to know for certain. We offer comprehensive inspection services using EPA-approved methods.

How long does the process take?

Timeline varies based on property size and contamination extent. A typical single-family home takes 2-5 days. We provide detailed timelines during initial assessment.

Are you licensed in multiple states?

We're fully licensed and certified in Maine and Massachusetts with all required insurance and bonding. New Hampshire licensing incoming.

What makes your services eco-friendly?

We use sustainable practices including proper waste recycling, non-toxic solutions, and methods that minimize environmental impact while meeting all EPA requirements.
